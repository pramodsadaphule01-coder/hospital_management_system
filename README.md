About the Project

The Hospital Management System is designed to streamline day-to-day operations of a medical facility. It provides modules for:

Registration and management of patients

Appointment scheduling and monitoring

Staff and doctor management

Billing and payment tracking

Reports and analytics

This system aims to reduce paperwork, improve efficiency and provide a clearer view of hospital workflow.

Usage

Patients: Create new patient records; view, update, or delete existing patients.

Doctors/Staff: Add staff credentials, assign roles, manage their schedules.

Appointments: Book, reschedule or cancel appointments for patients with doctors.

Billing: Generate bills for services provided; track payments and outstanding amounts.

Reports: Use search/filters to extract meaningful data (e.g., patients per doctor, daily revenue).
